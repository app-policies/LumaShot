Privacy Policy for LumaShot

Last updated: July 22, 2026

LumaShot is an on-device camera and photo editor for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

No LumaShot Account

LumaShot does not require an account, login, or user registration. LumaShot does not sell personal information.

Photos and Camera

LumaShot processes photos and camera data on your device only. Photos are not uploaded to external servers.

  • Camera access is used only to let you take photos in the app.
  • Photo library access is used only to import photos you choose and save edited photos back to your library.
  • Photo editing and filter processing happen on your device.

Advertising

The free version of LumaShot may display banner ads and limited interstitial ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may process device, advertising, and ad interaction information to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Depending on your device settings and Google's services, AdMob may process information such as approximate location, device identifiers, product interaction, advertising data, crash data, and performance data. Approximate location, device identifiers, product interaction, and advertising data may be used for third-party advertising and tracking. Crash data and performance data may be used for app functionality and service reliability.

Subscriptions and Purchases

LumaShot offers optional auto-renewable LumaShot Pro subscriptions through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Purchases are processed by Apple. LumaShot uses RevenueCat to provide subscription status, purchase, and restore functionality.

RevenueCat may process an anonymous app user identifier, purchase and subscription information, app and device information, and IP address to validate purchases and provide subscription access. LumaShot does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Local Editing Data

Editing settings, recipes, and related app preferences may be stored locally on your device so that edits and preferences can be restored. LumaShot does not use cloud synchronization for this data.

Third-Party Services

LumaShot uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob and User Messaging Platform for advertising and consent management.
  • RevenueCat for subscription entitlement and purchase status management.
  • Apple App Store and In-App Purchase services for payment processing.

LumaShot does not use external photo processing services, and photos are not uploaded to external servers.

AI Features

LumaShot does not use AI features to process your photos.

Children's Privacy

LumaShot does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
