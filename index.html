Privacy Policy for LumaShot

Last updated: July 7, 2026

LumaShot is a simple camera and photo filter app for iPhone. This Privacy Policy explains how LumaShot handles information when you use the app.

No Personal Data Collection

LumaShot does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or user registration.

Photos and Camera

LumaShot processes photos and camera data on your device only. Photos are not uploaded to external servers.

  • Camera access is used only to let you take photos in the app.
  • Photo library access is used only to import photos you choose and save edited photos back to your library.
  • Photo editing and filter processing happen on your device.

No Tracking or Analytics

LumaShot does not track you across apps or websites. The app does not collect analytics data or crash data.

No Ads, Purchases, or AI Features

LumaShot does not include advertising, in-app purchases, subscriptions, or AI features.

Third-Party Services

LumaShot does not use third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, cloud sync, or external photo processing services.

Children's Privacy

LumaShot does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@passmate.jp.
